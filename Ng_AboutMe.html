I am a student and I want to be a programmer. I have a family of 5 people. I love programming and making things, I've created a marionette thats controlled from a box with buttons out of cardboard. I can also play the violin and compose some music, I have learned the violin for 7 years. I like to play survival, co-op, and openworld games.
I used to create board games out of cardboard by thinking of an idea, creating the rules, designing the pieces, cutting them out, then finalizing. All of my games are played with 2 or more people and I would just play with my family. Since every piece was moved by hand, they would all have to be turn-based games to prevent any cheating or dissagreement.
